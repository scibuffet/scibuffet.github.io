What is SciBuffet?

SciBuffet is a platform for free educational content. In particular, we focus on collecting and developing legally free textbooks for university students. Other than free textbooks, we also gather helpful playlists or standalone videos or articles, so that a learner can find all the free materials for a topic, all organised in a single place.
